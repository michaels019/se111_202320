Review of The Accountant

Michael Sheehan

January 31st 2023

The Accountant was truly a work of art in my opinion. It was never a movie concept that I would've thought of, but I was amazed at every minute detail thatt they managed to slip into it. I would strongly recommend this movie to anyone who likes action movies, however, if you are not fond of blood, stay away.

The Accountant Trivia

  • The paintings featured in the film are: "Woman with Parasol and Child" by Renoir, 1874; "A Friend in Need" (Dogs Playing Poker) by Cassius Coolidge, 1903; and "Free Form" by Jackson Pollock, 1946.
  • "Where fun goes to die" is an actual unofficial motto of the University of Chicago.
  • The martial art mainly on display is a variety of Indonesian Pencak Silat, which is also seen in The Raid: Redemption (2011).
